# Chapter 902: Unseen Case-Based Passage

This unit tests Reading Skills via an unseen case-based factual passage (300-350 words) with visual inputs like statistical data, charts, or reports.
Questions should be a mix of MCQ, objective type, and short answers to test interpretation, analysis, and inference.
Passage topics should be interesting and age-appropriate (e.g., student survey statistics on screen time, sports participation, reading habits, environmental facts).
All data presented in the passage must be consistent and fully sufficient to answer the questions.
Vocab questions must test word meanings directly in the context of the passage.
