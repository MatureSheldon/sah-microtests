# Chapter 913: Reported Speech

This unit tests Grammar skills specifically on Reported Speech, focusing on CBSE Class 9 syllabus topics:
- Commands and requests
- Statements
- Questions
- Tense shifts and change in pronouns/time markers.

Questions should use realistic and meaningful contexts rather than isolated or artificial drills (e.g. dialogue completion between two students, reporting a message to a teacher).
Difficulty should be appropriate for Class 9.
Provide clear step-by-step grammatical explanations for correct reporting of tenses, pronouns, and verbs.
